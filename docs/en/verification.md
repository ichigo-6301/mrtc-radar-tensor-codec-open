# Verification

The verification chain covers MATLAB vector generation, the C bit-exact reference model, DPI-C invocation, and SystemVerilog RTL regression. Published legal vectors cover RAW_BYPASS, ZERO_RICE, DELTA_RICE, multi-block handling, AXI packing, loopback, backpressure, and malformed-stream negative conditions.

Published results apply to the recorded vectors and configurations. They are not exhaustive formal proof or coverage closure.
